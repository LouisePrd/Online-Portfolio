MARI-E

Explore the world of artist Marie Laurencin through a 360° augmented reality experience.

Project type: Website
Date: 2023
Context: Group project at Gobelins
Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WebXR, ThreeJS

Description:
Discover the life of Marie Laurencin through the painting "Les Biches," a tribute to her journey as a pioneering female artist of her time—painter, writer, and lesbian. Today's technology allows us to reinterpret the contemporary, to extend and subvert works of art to create a living, poetic canvas that incorporates the artist's graphic style.

Using your smartphone or tablet: stand in front of the painting to scan it. Click on the white shape and the show begins! Headphones are recommended for the best experience.

Dive into Marie Laurencin's world through a 360° augmented reality experience: discover her story, her career, and her works in a world halfway between painting and artificial intelligence.
