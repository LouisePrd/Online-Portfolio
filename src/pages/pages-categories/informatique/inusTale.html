Inu's Tale

Interactive game developed with Godot for a Gobelins project.

Project type: Interactive game
Date: 2023
Context: Student project at Gobelins, in a group of 3 developers.
Technologies: Godot, GDScript

Description:
In a small village on the outskirts of Kyoto, a matsuri (Japanese festival) is being celebrated. Following a ritual, an inugami and a human find themselves bound to one another, but a disaster occurs, causing many yōkai to enter the human world.

They must cooperate to bring our yōkai protagonist back to his world, but time is running out: if the inugami does not return in time, he will have to take possession of the human's body and live as such under the threat of malevolent yōkai. Or even worse, perish.
