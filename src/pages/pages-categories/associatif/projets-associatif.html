Associative Projects

GIC

The GIC is the association of creative engineers at ESIEE Paris.
We connect the arts, sciences, and student life by organizing projects that bring people together and leave a special lasting impression.

President – Oct. 2024 to June 2025

As president, I invested myself in growing the association and making it more vibrant than ever, particularly with the recent merge between Gustave Eiffel University and ESIEE Paris.

Some notable events from the year:

  • IMACannes: our annual ceremony that celebrates creativity, writing, mastery of audiovisual imagery, editing, post-production, and special effects by students in the IMAC program. Complete organization of the event: communication, logistics, film selection, jury, ceremony.
  • The End-of-Year Gala: the grand closing night for all graduating classes, which I coordinate with the team.
  • The JeudiMACs: regular get-togethers to build bonds between graduating classes through fun and creative activities.

Events Coordinator - July 2024 to January 2025

Before becoming president, I was the events manager, responsible for creating, coordinating, and organizing GIC events with the team. Student life can sometimes be intimidating, especially at a large school like ESIEE, and I wanted to help create a welcoming environment through team-building events and creative collaborations.

We organized the Journées de Cohésion at the beginning of the year, as well as themed weekly afterworks. These evenings included various activities such as the “Jeu du Killer”, a makeup contest, and a Christmas workshop focused on creation, sharing, and festive atmosphere. The idea was to encourage exchanges, stimulate creativity, and create a real community around creativity in engineering..

GIC in a few pictures

Cohesion Days: these days are a traditional cohesion event for students at ESIEE Paris, particularly for creative engineers, organized by the GIC. A time to forge links between classes and encourage creativity on the theme of Harry Potter.

IMACannes: the annual ceremony that celebrates creativity, writing, mastery of audiovisual imaging, editing, post-production, and special effects by students in the IMAC program and members of the association.

The end-of-year gala: a grand evening celebrating the achievements of all graduating classes.
